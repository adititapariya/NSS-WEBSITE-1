About NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a youth program launched by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports of India in 1969. It aims to develop the personality and character of students through community service. NSS has a motto of "Not me, but you" which reflects the need for selfless service towards others.

How NSS Works

NSS functions through various educational institutions and provides opportunities for the students to engage in community service. NSS volunteers work on various projects such as blood donation camps, literacy programs, health camps, and environmental protection. NSS encourages active involvement of students in community work and provides them with a platform to contribute to the society.

Committees in NSS

NSS has various committees that oversee different aspects of its functioning. Some of the important committees are:

  • Planning and Advisory Committee
  • Programme and Activities Committee
  • Volunteers Management Committee
  • Finance Committee
  • Training and Orientation Committee
  • Publicity and Med1ia Committee
  • Community Outreach Committee

Work Done by NSS

NSS has been involved in various community service activities over the years. Some of the notable work done by NSS includes:

  • Organizing blood donation camps and eye donation drives
  • Conducting awareness campaigns on health, hygiene, and sanitation
  • Providing education to underprivileged children through literacy programs
  • Undertaking environmental protection and conservation projects
  • Assisting in disaster management and relief efforts
